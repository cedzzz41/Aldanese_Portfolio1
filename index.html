NichroDry

Energy-Efficient Small-Scale Paddy Dryer with Nichrome Coil Heating Element & Rotating Cylindrical Drum

Designed and fabricated an energy-efficient paddy dryer leveraging a Nichrome coil as the primary heating element, paired with an innovative rotating cylindrical drum mechanism. The system reduces drying time and energy consumption significantly compared to conventional sun-drying — aimed at improving post-harvest efficiency for small-scale Filipino farmers.

PharmAssist

Automated Pill Dispenser & Reminder System with Biometric Authentication

Engineered an automated pill dispenser featuring biometric fingerprint authentication to ensure secure, identity-verified medication access. The system also includes a programmable reminder mechanism — addressing medication adherence and safety concerns, particularly for elderly users who manage multiple prescriptions.
